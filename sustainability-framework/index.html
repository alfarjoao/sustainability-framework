Our Methodology

Built on comprehensive lifecycle assessment research and multi-variable sensitivity analysis

This framework evaluates renovation versus demolition decisions through systematic whole-life carbon analysis. Our approach considers embodied carbon, operational energy performance, material reuse potential, and lifecycle implications across multiple building scenarios.

Detailed methodology documentation provided by Ms. Valentine Lhoest will be integrated here, including formula explanations, variable definitions, and research references.

About This Project

Academic Research Tool

Developed to support evidence-based decision-making in sustainable building practices

This tool was developed as part of a master's thesis examining the carbon implications of building renovation versus demolition decisions. The framework synthesizes current research in lifecycle assessment and provides practitioners with accessible, evidence-based guidance.
